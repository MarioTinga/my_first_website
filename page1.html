What is D&D?

D&D is a TTRPG, an acronym for "Table Top Role Play Game". Foundamentally is a table top game but different from everything you have ever tried. You gather with your friends and join them in any kind of fantasy adventure you can possibly imagine. With the aid of a bit of math and the power of your imagination, you can visit wonderful places and meet any kind of creature!

Why should I play D&D?

D&D is a game made for anyone of any age who wants to embark on a magical journey with friends. This journey could take you and your friends just hours to complete or even months, there are groups who play a campaign for years, but you should not get overwhelmed by this, a game of D&D is suitable even fo a short campaign which could last just a few hours. It could be a perfect game for a different game night with your friends and family!

How do I play D&D?

Playing D&D can seem overwhelming at first, but don't be scared! The sheer quantity of material and possibility you have at your fingertips could seem confusing, but it's ok. You need to start small. You could start by only buying the starter set and try to give it a go. There are options even if you don't want to spend any money. There are a lot of resources available for free on the internet, just look up a simple one shot adventure and the basic rules of the game and you are set!
